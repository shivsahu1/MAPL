Services

Our core competency lies in executing time bound turnkey projects covering the gamut of power transmission business with the highest quality. Our services include design, procurement, supply, installation/ erection, testing and commissioning of T&D systems such as lines, substations and distribution centers. We also offer end to end external power supply and grid connectivity solutions for industrial plants.

In a nutshell, our major industry experience includes:

  • EHV transmission and HT lines
  • EHV and MV substations
  • Distribution transformer centres and LT lines

Besides, we also provide our specialized expertise for technical consultation, power loss reduction and grid connectivity of solar and wind power plants.

For details, please refer to projects accomplished by MAEIPL.
