Chairman’s message

MAEIPL is now into its 3rd decade of business, and can proudly claim to be one of the best EPC company in Power T&D in Maharashtra.

Having maintained an unmatched level of quality and customer satisfaction across all projects despite a fast accelerating business, we are now well equipped to meet the needs of India’s rapidly growing power sector. We have not only strived to add value to our clients but also provided extensive growth opportunities for people within organization. The result- MAEIPL ensures better solutions, better people and better relations.

At MAEIPL, “In & Out” safety is given the topmost priority – ‘In’ pertaining to our employees working in potentially fatal circumstances, and ‘Out’ referring of course, to the public- the end, and most valued consumers of our services. Since the day we started out, we have tried to exceed the expectations of our last customer- the ultimate user of electricity. We have constantly maintained and upgraded quality through innovation. The numerous accolades and more importantly, the faith bestowed on us by customers are nothing but testimonials to the safety of our practices and the quality of our services.

While this success is indeed encouraging, we look at it as a platform to aim for higher peaks. We have always believed in asset generation for the nation by building the most reliable power infrastructure. Having built and nurtured a 1500-strong (and counting) workforce to become the best in class, we have also contributed our share to the development of our society.

Looking ahead, our target is to achieve a healthy growth each year, and be counted amongst the nation’s top companies in the power transmission and distribution sector. As MAEIPL systematically ascends to new heights, we sincerely look forward to work with you and contribute to your success.
