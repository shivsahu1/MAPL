Company

Maha Active Engineers India Pvt. Ltd., is a leading provider of reliable and world class infrastructure in power transmission and distribution. Established in 1990, we are well equipped to meet the needs of the rapidly growing power sector.

We, a group of qualified and vastly experienced engineers, are committed to provide the entire services including design, engineering, supply, erection, testing, commissioning and handing over schemes. A customer centric approach and a continuous quest for world class quality has enabled us to build long lasting relations with our customers. We have served Maharashtra state utilities for over three decades and are well known at all levels for our performance and values.

Here at MAEIPL, we idolize electricity. We believe that the power sector will act as the most critical factors in shaping the future of our country. And we take a great pleasure and pride in serving this sector with this belief. Having been involved in a huge number of high profile T&D projects, the company has developed the expertise in the industry.

Maha Active Engineers India Pvt.Ltd. has established ,documented and maintained a Quality Management System and continually improved its effectiveness with the requirements of International Standard ISO 9001:2008.
