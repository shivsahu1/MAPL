Team

Our Team

It is our people who provide the innovations that enable us, and consequently our customers stay ahead in one of the country’s fastest moving markets. We are blessed with a team of qualified, competent and professional engineers and designers, each one of whom is a specialist in his/ her field of activity. Moreover, the team has a unique, and probably ideal blend of youth and experience which has enabled MAEIPL to achieve its current standing in the industry. Our workforce comprises 1500 skilled labourers led by 80 supervisors.

Besides the technical personnel, MAEIPL is armed with the most efficient support staff that ably carries out all background operational activities, allowing our technicians to function to their full potential. Indeed, MAEIPL owes its success to its team’s unwavering dedication and fire to build the company into a multi-faceted organization.
Added to this, we are led and motivated by our top management, which comprises distinguished individuals acknowledged for their education, expertise and most of all, their integrity. The informal management hierarchy and participative decision making prevalent in the organization has helped MAEIPL march forward with a unified vision.
