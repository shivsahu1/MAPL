Company Profile

Maha Active Engineers India Pvt. Ltd., a provider of reliable and world class infrastructure in transmission and distribution of power has completed 20 years of services and is poised to make rapid strides in the upcoming years. As a group of dynamic, progressive and committed people, we offer the entire range of EPC services covering design, engineering, supply, construction, testing and commissioning of MV and EHV infrastructure.

True to our motto “Serving Electricity” , we treat electricity as our Goddess. With a firm belief that power will be one of the most critical factors in shaping the future of our country, we take great pleasure and pride in serving this sector. Our primary focus is to ensure better utilization of the power generated by delivering lasting transmission and distribution network.

Having successfully executed a number of high profile T&D projects and gained expertise in the power industry, the organization is set-up to deliver projects with diversity in both nature and volume of work. A qualified and experienced team of engineers and managers with a customer-centric approach, a continuous quest for quality has enabled MAEIPL to gain immense customer faith and develop excellent relations with state board/utility officials over the years.
